# Privacy Policy for Symboliq AI meeting assistant **Last Updated: [INSERT DATE, e.g., May 2026]** Symboliq AI ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. Unlike traditional AI assistants, Symboliq AI is built on a **"Privacy-by-Design"** architecture where all data processing occurs strictly on your local device. ## 1. Zero Data Collection Symboliq AI does **not** collect, store, or transmit any of the following data to external servers: * **Audio Recordings:** All meeting audio stays in your device's local storage. * **Transcripts:** Speech-to-text conversion is performed on-device. * **Summaries:** Meeting insights are generated locally by the LLM. * **Personal Information:** We do not require account registration, email addresses, or name collection to function. ## 2. On-Device AI Processing (Offline) The App leverages the hardware acceleration of your device (Apple A18 Neural Engine or Intel Arc GPU) to run large-scale machine learning models (Whisper and Qwen 2.5). * **No Cloud APIs:** We do not use OpenAI, Google Cloud, or any third-party cloud services. * **Airplane Mode Compatible:** The App's core functionality is available without an internet connection. ## 3. Local Storage and Deletion All meeting data is stored in the App's local directory: * Users have full control over their data. * Deleting a meeting within the App permanently removes the associated audio and text files from the device storage. * Uninstalling the App will delete all locally stored meeting data. ## 4. Third-Party Services * **RevenueCat:** We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions and purchases. RevenueCat may collect anonymous transaction identifiers to verify your Pro status. No meeting data is ever shared with RevenueCat. * **Telemetry:** The App does not include third-party analytics or tracking SDKs. ## 5. Security Because your data never leaves your device, it is protected by your operating system's built-in security (iOS Data Protection or Windows BitLocker). We recommend using strong device passcodes and biometric locks. ## 6. Changes to This Policy We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time.
